Cue Demo Project Description

Cue is a blind-first Mac work operator for safe, user-initiated desktop work.
The demo uses local files only: a project brief, a synthetic contract, a local
dashboard, a weakly labeled form, the official hackathon PDF copy, TextEdit, and
Terminal.

What to show:
- Cue opens local work materials and summarizes them.
- Cue proposes short workflows before acting.
- State-changing actions require confirmation.
- Terminal handoff remains read-only by default.
- The app surfaces verification, policy tier, redacted audit status, and
  Cerebras timing without exposing secrets.

No real customer data, credentials, private documents, or account details are
included in these assets.
